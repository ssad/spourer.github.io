This is SmartPourer

A smart pouring spout that controls consumption of alcohol and entertains you and your friends. Smart Pourer keeps your health safe and stable.

Time To Drive

SmartPourer informs you in advance about the time you can get behind the wheel after drinking. Set the presumable driving time and the device will calculate and limit your alcohol consumption.

Drinking Games

SmartPourer is the best device for any alcohol occasion. Thanks to the entertainment section in our App, which contains different games and quizzes, you can have a lot of fun spending quality leisure time with your friends.

Perfect Cocktail

SmartPourer takes mixing of cocktails at home to the next level. The device provides recipes, automatically identifies poured alcohol and measures its amount in the right proportions.

Keep The Track Of Events

Be drink responsible

SmartPourer keeps track of your condition and alerts about oncoming intoxication.

A Party To Remember

SmartPourer accurately calculates appropriate limits of alcohol according to your physiological parameters and maintains the alcohol balance.

Home Bar

SmartPourer identifies any alcohol drink and shows the residue of spirit in a bottle.
